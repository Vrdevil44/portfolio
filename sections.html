My Story

My journey into technology began with a fascination for how things work. From disassembling electronics to building my first computer, I was driven by curiosity and a desire to create. This natural inclination led me to pursue formal education in Computer Information Systems at the University of the Fraser Valley.

Chapter 2: Finding My Path

Early in my career, I discovered my passion for web development while working with Asia Jaunt and Raj Speed Honda. These experiences taught me the importance of user-centered design and the impact of digital presence on business success. I honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also developing an eye for design and user experience.

Chapter 3: Expanding Horizons

My role as Interim COO at Coli Spaces pushed me beyond technical skills into business strategy and leadership. Managing teams, implementing CRM systems, and driving marketing campaigns broadened my perspective on how technology serves business objectives. This experience taught me to see beyond code to the larger ecosystem of business needs.

At Arini Data Services, I deepened my technical expertise by working with enterprise clients like Tesla and JD Sports. Network infrastructure, full-stack development, and on-site technical support became part of my expanding skill set. Today, as an IT Helpdesk Intern at Archway Community Services, I'm applying my technical knowledge to support community programs while continuing my education. I'm passionate about creating technology that serves people, whether through elegant code, intuitive interfaces, or reliable support.

Chapter 6: The Future

Looking ahead, I aim to combine my technical skills, design sensibility, and business understanding to create digital experiences that make a difference. Whether developing innovative applications, designing intuitive interfaces, or building robust systems, my goal is to use technology to solve real problems and enhance people's lives.
